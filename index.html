Eron Lloyd is a graduate student at Lewis University, completing a Master of Science in Data Science. His research interests are in public choice theory, electoral systems, and voting technology. Before he began his graduate studies, Eron served in the trenches of government, where he experienced the good, bad, and ugly sides of public service. Through his research work, he plans to concentrate on better understanding the breakdown of our democratic institutions and helping to create solutions to repair them.
